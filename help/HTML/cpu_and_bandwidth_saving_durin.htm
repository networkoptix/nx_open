CPU and Bandwidth Saving during Playback (RADASS)

HD Wittess provides the powerful feature that allows saving a lot of CPU and network bandwidth. The key of the feature is the ability to acquire multiple streams from a single camera: High quality (regular) and ow quality (10 times less). If viewing a camera ant there is not enough bandwidth between client and server to display the high quality stream, then camera is automaticall switched by low stream video.

On the other hand the client may not have sufficient CPU to display the whole bunch of videos simultaneously (either there are more than 20 Full HD video streams or a user is trying to perform fast forward on 16x on several Full HD videos). In this case cameras are switched in low-quality stream. As a result CPU is loaded much more optimal and playback is much more smoother.

Note that this feature requires support of dual-streaming on the camera.
