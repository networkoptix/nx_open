Editing Recording Schedule

DW Spectrum provides powerful and flexible schedule for configuring recording parameters.

Specific date and time can be set in the recording schedule as well.

Examples:

•Workdays 8AM-8PM – record Always, 24 FPS, high quality

•Weekends and Workdays 8PM-8AM – record motion only, 10 fps, low quality.

The cameras recorded by Media Servers are marked with a small red circle in Resource Tree:

- a camera is being recorded

- recording is set up for the camera, but it's not recorded right now (a license is still used).

To set up recording schedule:

1.Open camera settings and go to Recording:

Viewers are not permitted to make any changes to the recording schedule.

2.Click Enable Recording. Check the number of licenses used. If the number is insufficient, click Get More Licenses and do activation.

Click on Display FPS on Grid and Display Quality on Grid to show/or hide FPS and quality in grid cells.

3.Select a Recording Mode:

•Record Always

•Motion Only – Recording will start if motion occurs. To specify how much time before and after motion should be recorded use Record before motion and Record after motion selectors. Motion detection should be supported by the camera (Software or Hardware).

•Motion + Low quality always – low quality stream is recorded always and high quality stream is acquired only if motion occurs. To use this recording type camera should support dual-streaming

•Never.

4.Select a recording quality.

 

5.Select FPS.

As soon as all the parameters are selected, click on Schedule Grid and time range.

•Click and drag to select multiple cells

•Click on hour to select an entire column

•Click on weekday to select an entire row

•Click on the top left corner of Schedule Grid to select all cells

Note: to select same values, hold Alt and click on a cell to choose a recording mode, FPS and quality values.

6.Repeat to set another recording for different cells.

7.If Motion recording is set it is possible to adjust Pre-recording and Post Recording intervals. That's the time the camera will be recorded before motion starts and after it ends.

8.Click Apply or OK when finished. To discard, changes click Cancel.

In case of insufficient licenses, the following warning will appear:

Enable Recording flag will be disabled; however, all schedule settings will be saved.

Example: Recording Settings view:

•Workdays 8AM-8PM – record Always, 24 FPS, high quality

•Weekends and Workdays 8PM-8AM – record motion only, 10 fps, low quality.
