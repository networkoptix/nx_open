Exporting Recorded Region from Single Camera

DW Spectrum supports exporting in several media formats: Matroska (mkv) and avi. These formats are supported by most players (additional codecs may be needed for playback).

Export can be done even for local video files.

To export:

1.Select the desired camera on Scene.

2.Select the region to export on Time Scale:

•Use Right Click Drag

•Open Context Menu on Time Scale and click Mark Selection Start (or press “[”), then Mark Selection Start (or press “]”)

•To clear selection use the corresponding Context Menu item

3.Adjust the region to export by dragging edges

To make zooming easier, navigate through Timeline using a mouse wheel.

4.Right-click for Context Menu and choose Export Selected Range:

If the selection does not contain archive data this feature is not available.

5.Choose the desired file name, format and location and click Save (Cancel will close the dialog and any exported data will not be saved):

It is possible to perform standard files and folders operations in this form (like in Windows Explorer).

IMPORTANT: AVI and MKV formats are supported. AVI is more widely used but the codec remains intact (H264). To view exported videos in other players may require additional codecs. Additionally some codecs are not allowed in AVI format. In this case warning will be provided.

 

As soon as export is finished, the video will be available in local files:

IMPORTANT: this resource will be available until restart of the client. To make it available permanently, save the exported video to DW Spectrum Media Folder (see “Configuring Media Folders”).
