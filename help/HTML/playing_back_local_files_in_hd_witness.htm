Playing Back Local Files in DW Spectrum

In addition to powerful camera playback engine DW Spectrum can act as media center: it can play back almost any file. Any codec and any container are supported.

Additionally it is possible to export fragments from video files and to take screenshots.

IMPORTANT: some video files may contain 5.1 sound stream. In order to play back those files on stereo speakers: open Main Menu and choose System Settings then check Downmix Audio from 5.1 to 2.1 channels. After restart the sound will be played correctly.
