Panic Recording

This feature allows switching recording settings for all cameras the recording is setup on to highest available FPS and quality.  This feature may be activated by any user.

Note that is a recording is not enabled for a camera, Panic Recording will not be applied to it.

To do so just press on the top right corner.

Current Panic Recording status can be viewed:

on the Panic button – it becomes red if panic recording is on ().

on the recording schedule.

On a server's settings.

To stop panic recording press again. The recording settings will return to the values previously specified.
