Using Thumbnails for Better Navigation

Thumbnails are fast and convenient way to navigate through archive. Thumbnails represent a single shots from archived videos that are displayed on the Timeline. This is used to better identify events in a particular moment.

To open thumbnails:

1.Click on the desired camera.

2.Click on

button or point a mouse cursor on the top of the Timeline, click and pull it up:

Thumbnails panel can be resized. To resize, point a mouse cursor  over tTime tline, click and scrow up or down with a mouse wheel:

The white dots under the picture point on the exact archive place where the shot has been taken. When clicking on a thumbnail, the current playback position will jump to the corresponding spot in the archive.

To close thumbnails, press

again or pull the thumbnails panel down.
