Checking the Validity of Exported Videos

Watermark allows users to check for validity of footage to determine whether or not any modifications were performed to native footage. Any videos exported by DW Spectrum (eitther single cameras or Multi-Channel videos) can be checked for validity.

To check the validity of exported videos:

Bring the desired video onto the scene.

Right-click on Context Menu and select Check File Watermark (or press Ctrl + C)
