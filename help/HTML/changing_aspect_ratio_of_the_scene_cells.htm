Changing Aspect Ratio of Scene Cells

Some Cameras provide video in 4:3 or 16:9 format. This feature is used in order to occupy Scene space efficiently: cells’ aspect ratio must be the same as Cameras’ one.

To select aspect ratio use Change Cell Aspect Ratio Context Menu item:
