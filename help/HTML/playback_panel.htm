Playback Panel

DW Spectrum provides powerful and intuitive playback system. It includes seamless live to archive transition, smooth archive playback, extensive search capabilities, local files playback etc. All playback operations can be performed within the Playback Panel:

The following elements are displayed there:

•Playback Buttons – used for playback of selected Item(s)

•Speed Slider – controls the playback speed of selected Item(s)

•UNREGISTERED EVALUATION VERSION – represents the archived data for selected Item(s)

•Volume Control – used to adjust volume for selected Item

•Additional Buttons:

• – switches selected camera(s) to live playback or indicates that they are playing live. See "Navigating through Archive and Live"

• – performs a time synchronization of all cameras displayed on Scene. See "UNREGISTERED EVALUATION VERSION"

• – opens a calendar to help navigating through archive (see "Using Calendar")

• – expands Time Line and shows small previews called Thumbnails. See "Using Thumbnails for Better Navigation".
