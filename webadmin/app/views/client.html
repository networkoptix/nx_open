Welcome to Lite Client

Lite Client can show up to 4 live videos,
and can be controlled either by keyboard and mouse connected to the device,
or through mobile app

By keyboard/mouse

Select cell or Single click
Switch camera in a cell or Wheel
Enter / exit fullscreen or Double click
Exit lite client
