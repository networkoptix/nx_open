Welcome to Nx1 Lite Client

After being launched, Lite Client can show up to 4 live videos,
and can be controlled either by keyboard or mouse connected to Nx1,
or through mobile app

By keyboard/mouse

Select cell or Single click
Switch camera in a cell or Wheel
Enter / exit fullscreen or Double click
Exit lite client

With mobile app

Install Nx Witness app via Google Play or App Store
Connect to system "System" (192.168.154.131:7001)
Select "Nx1" screen in side menu
Turn Lite Client on and select cameras to display
