Logging in to CMS

Click on the Log in button and enter your email and password to log in to CMS, log in to your company's Cloud Portal.

Check the Remember me checkbox to be automatically logged in when you open the site page.

If necessary, you can use the Forgot Password button to generate an email that will allow you to reset your password.

The CMS portal opens on the Cloud Portal landing page:

Cloud Portal Footer Shortcuts

Each Cloud Portal page has a set of footers that provide shortcuts to the landing (About {{Product Name}}), download, known limitations, support, terms (License Agreement) and privacy policy pages. (The Known Limitations page link {{CLOUD_ADDRESS}}/content/faq and content are hard-coded by Network Optix and cannot be edited from the CMS.)

Language menu

If you have requested that your customization support multiple languages, the footer will include a menu that lets users choose the display language for their Cloud Portal session.

Refer to "Selecting a language" for additional information concerning language display.

The email address you logged in with will be displayed in the upper-right corner. A menu below this provides the following links:

Account Settings

Opens a page where you can enter or edit your Email address, First name and Last name, and select a display Language. (To change the available languages contact Network Optix support; these cannot be updated from the CMS portal.)

Change Password

Opens a page where you can enter a new password by entering your current password.

Log Out

Click to log out of CMS and return to the Cloud portal landing page.

Portal Management

Click on this link to open the Cloud Administration dashboard.  The available applications, and options within those, depend on your access privileges.
