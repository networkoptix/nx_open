Portal Management Dashboard

The Portal Management link opens to the Cloud Administration dashboard.

There are three tabs on the Cloud Administration page:

Dashboard – provides links to editing and user applications.

Bookmarks – (Currently disabled.)

Applications – provides alternate links to Dashboard applications.

There are two links in the header:

View Site – click to open the current published Cloud Portal.

Log Out – click to exit CMS.

There is also a Contact us link that opens the Network Optix support page in a new browser tab, where you can submit a request for information or technical support.

In the Dashboard

•Content Managers have access to the Content Management application, which includes:

Pages – lets you edit, preview and submit pages for approval.

Revisions – a log of exactly what content changed, and when and by whom updates were approved.

•Portal Managers have access to the Content Management application – plus the additional authority to review and publish revisions, and also to the Cloud Users application, which includes:

Accounts – a list of all Cloud account users that can be sorted, searched, and exported.
