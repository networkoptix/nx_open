Privacy Policy page

If desired, use this page to enter personal data and privacy policies that apply to Cloud Portal users. The link to this page is {{portal url}}/content/privacy. Alternately, you can use the Privacy Policy link field on the branding page to link to an existing corporate privacy policy page.

All fields on this page are required.

Available fields are:

Language – select from the language pulldown menu. See Selecting a language.

Privacy policy header – enter the page title.

Privacy policy content – enter the terms of your corporate privacy policy. This is an HTML field.
